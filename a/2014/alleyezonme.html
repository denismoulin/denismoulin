Alleyezonme

Experimental, Ux, Ui, Spotify App

I’m a big fan of 2Pac. One night I was at a party and someone skipped a joyful explicit 2Pac song to play something awful (imagine a Bonnie Tyler, or whatever you hate) and it got me thinking...

I created a prototype for a Spotify plugin that could be the solution to that endless cacophony of competing amateur DJs (or just, you know, a way to make sure 2Pac gets his due).

Maybe there are already tons of apps like this one (if you know of one, let me know). Anyway, you’re one click away from getting free updates...like a thug.

This project is not done yet, I'll keep updating/add screens.

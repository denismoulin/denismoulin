Font Manager

A font is never finished until it’s used by the customer. In 2014 I worked with the Fontyou team under the lead of Gregori Vincens to think of a new way to consume and use fonts. The sync app is one of Fontyou’s key components.

This tool had to provide a new experience, but stay in parallel with the Fontyou platform, using the same codes and the same language.

For our application we could manipulate certain UX ../data familiar to the user. Here, we placed the list at the center of the user’s experience, differentiating the application from the platform.

TL;DR


Working on this synchro tool was challenging and rewarding, and it taught me a lot in terms of methodology in creating and improving signs and codes for multiple devices. It’s a great feeling to work on a project that you’ll use yourself. I loved working on this with the Fontyou team and I’m sure they’ll keep on rockin’ this subject!
